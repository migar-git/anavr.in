How to Build a $10K/Month Business with AI in 90 Days

Most entrepreneurs overcomplicate things. They spend months "planning" and never ship. Let me show you how AI changes the game completely.

In Q4 2025, I built and launched a micro-SaaS that hit $10K MRR in its third month. Zero employees. No VC funding. Just me, Claude, and a few well-chosen tools.

Here's exactly how I did it.

Week 1-2: Idea Validation (The Fast Way)

Forget surveys. Forget "customer interviews." Here's what I did:

  • Used ChatGPT to analyze 50+ Reddit threads in my niche
  • Scraped Twitter for pain points using Phantombuster
  • Built a landing page in 2 hours (Carrd + copy from Claude)
  • Ran $100 in Google Ads to see if anyone clicked "Pre-order"

Result: 47 email signups in 72 hours. Good enough. Ship it.

Week 3-6: Building the MVP

This is where AI shines. I used:

  • Claude Sonnet 4.5 for all the code (Next.js + Supabase)
  • v0.dev for UI components (saved weeks of design work)
  • Cursor IDE for rapid iteration
  • GitHub Copilot for boilerplate

I'm not a great developer. But with AI pair programming, I shipped a working SaaS in 3 weeks that would've taken a team 3 months.

Week 7-8: Launch & First Customers

Launched on Product Hunt, Hacker News, and in 5 relevant subreddits. No fancy PR. Just a good product that solved a real problem.

First month: $1,200 MRR. Not bad.

Week 9-12: Scaling with Automation

This is where most founders burn out. Not me. I automated everything:

  • Customer onboarding emails (n8n + GPT-4)
  • Support tickets (Crisp + AI chatbot)
  • Content marketing (automated blog posts + SEO)
  • Social media (3 months of posts scheduled)

By month 3, I was working 5 hours per week on the business. MRR: $10,400.

The Real Secret

It's not about the tools. It's about speed and focus.

Speed: AI lets you move 10x faster than traditional methods. Use that to your advantage.

Focus: Don't build features. Build solutions to specific pain points. One at a time.

Your 90-Day Blueprint

  1. Days 1-14: Find a painful problem and validate demand
  2. Days 15-45: Build an MVP using AI coding tools
  3. Days 46-60: Launch and get your first 10 paying customers
  4. Days 61-90: Automate operations and scale

That's it. No complicated funnels. No expensive agencies. Just you, AI, and a commitment to shipping fast.
